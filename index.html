GRAND JEEVAK HOTEL





Welcome to The Jeevak Great Eastern Bodhgaya

The JEEVAK Great Eastern Bodhgaya was built in 1840. and is the longest continuously operating luxury hotel in Bihar with 65 years of operation until its renovation in 2006. The architecture at this hotel is a combination of Victorian, Edwardian, and contemporary styles. One of the best luxury hotels in Bodhgaya, it is centrally located in close proximity to key commercial and business locations.

The JEEVAK Great Eastern Bodhgaya operates 50 rooms and suites and offers over 1,000 square feet of conference and banqueting space. The spa at this hotel is spread over 1,000 square feet and includes a salon.
